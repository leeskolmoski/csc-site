What We Do

CSC acts as a dedicated extension of your team. We provide Owner’s Representative services, Construction Management (CM), and General Management (GM) support, with a focus on safety, schedule, quality, and cost control. We coordinate design and permitting, oversee contractors, and drive commissioning and turnover to operations.

Owner’s Representative

Single point of accountability on the owner’s side—aligning scope, budget, and schedule; running due diligence; leading developer/GC selection; and overseeing delivery.

Construction Management (CM)

Planning and coordination from preconstruction through closeout: logistics, long-lead tracking, change management, contractor integration, and field oversight.

General Management (GM)

Executive-level direction for multi-site or complex programs—governance, decision frameworks, stakeholder communication, and risk mitigation.

Design & Permitting

Define requirements; coordinate architects/engineers; manage submittals; and navigate permitting, utilities, and inspections to keep the path clear.

MEP Coordination

Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing (MEP) integration—requirements, interfaces, and commissioning for reliable operations.

QA/QC

Quality Assurance / Quality Control (QA/QC) plans, inspections, and punchlist management to meet specifications and performance criteria.

Safety Leadership

Site-specific safety plans, training, audits, and incident prevention woven into daily execution—safety is built in, not bolted on.

Commissioning & Turnover

Functional testing, documentation, and training to ensure a smooth handoff to operations—with clear responsibilities and closeout records.
