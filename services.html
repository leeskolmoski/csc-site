Our Services

Full lifecycle support across development, design, construction, and handover.

Design and pre-construction

  • Program management, site investigation, and due diligence.
  • Conceptual design to estimate to schedule.
  • Select and negotiate with architects and engineers on behalf of the owner.
  • Coordinate the design team to maintain scope, schedule, and budget.
  • Coordinate permits with municipality agencies.
  • Pre qualify contractors and assist with selection and negotiations.

Construction

  • Participate in GC procurement and monitor on site progress for quality, safety, and schedule.
  • Review and approve pay applications. Track monthly cost updates.
  • Coordinate tenant improvements and final documentation with municipality and owner.

Post construction

  • Oversee contract closeout and handover to operations.
  • Commission building systems and equipment.
  • Oversee training for management and maintenance personnel.
