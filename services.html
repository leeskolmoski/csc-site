Services and Roles

We support the entire project lifecycle. From early planning and design management to construction oversight and final commissioning, we ensure projects are safe, compliant, on schedule, and within budget.

Design and Pre-construction

Early decisions save the most money. We manage site investigations, due diligence, and concept design to set each project on the right path. Our team coordinates with architects, engineers, and municipal agencies so plans are complete and permits are secured before work begins.

  • Program management and feasibility studies
  • Concept design, estimating, and scheduling
  • Architect and engineer selection and contract negotiation
  • Permit and approval coordination with municipalities
  • Bidder pre-qualification, procurement, and contractor negotiations

Construction

During construction, we represent the owner's interests at all times. We actively monitor quality control, verify that safety procedures are followed, and ensure any deficiencies are corrected quickly. Our team tracks progress against the schedule, reviews contractor pay applications, and provides leadership with clear monthly updates.

  • GC procurement participation and on-site oversight
  • Continuous quality control monitoring
  • Safety procedure checks and follow-up on corrections
  • Review and approval of pay applications and expenditures
  • Monthly cost tracking and reporting
  • Coordination of tenant improvements and documentation

Post-construction

Our work continues through closeout and beyond. We finalize contracts, commission systems, and train operations and maintenance staff so your facility performs as designed from day one.

  • Contract closeout and turnover to operations
  • Building and equipment commissioning
  • Training for management and maintenance teams
