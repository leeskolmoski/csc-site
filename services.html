Services and Roles

We support the full project lifecycle — from due diligence and design, through construction, commissioning, and final turnover to operations.

How we operate

CSC acts as your Owner’s Representative, providing Construction Management (CM) and General Management (GM) leadership across schedule, cost, quality, and safety. We coordinate Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing (MEP) integration, implement Quality Assurance / Quality Control (QA/QC), and manage the General Contractor (GC) and trade partners to deliver outcomes with precision and care.

Pre-construction

Program & Due Diligence

  • Program management, site investigation, and feasibility.
  • Budgeting and baseline schedule with risk allowances.
  • Lease/developer options, incentives, and utilities strategy.

Design Coordination

  • Requirements definition and basis of design.
  • Architect/engineer onboarding and design reviews.
  • MEP criteria, process utilities, and code compliance.

Permitting & Procurement

  • Permits, approvals, and agency coordination.
  • Bid strategy, scopes, and GC/major trade selection.
  • Long-lead identification and early release packages.

Construction

Field & Safety Leadership

  • Daily coordination, logistics, and site access control.
  • Project-specific safety program and audits.
  • Workface planning to maintain quality and flow.

Controls & Change Management

  • Schedule tracking, cost control, and cash-flow.
  • RFI/submittal management and document control.
  • Change evaluation and stakeholder approvals.

MEP / Process Integration

  • MEP coordination and utility interfaces.
  • Equipment/vendor integration and factory acceptance.
  • QA/QC inspections and deficiency tracking.

Post-construction

Commissioning

  • Functional testing and performance verification.
  • Start-up sequencing with vendors and the GC.
  • Issues list, resolution, and acceptance sign-off.

Turnover to Operations

  • O&M manuals, as-builts, and training.
  • Warranty coordination and closeout documents.
  • Stabilization support during ramp-up.

Program Reporting

  • Final cost and schedule reporting.
  • Lessons learned and risk closeout.
  • Recommendations for future sites.
