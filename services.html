Services and Roles

We support the full project lifecycle, from program management and due diligence to commissioning and handover.

Design and Pre-construction

  • Program management, site investigation, due diligence.
  • Concept design, estimating, and scheduling.
  • Select and negotiate with architects and engineers for the owner.
  • Coordinate the design team to scope, schedule, and budget.
  • Work with municipal agencies for required permits and approvals.
  • Pre-qualify bidders, run procurement, assist with contractor negotiations.

Construction

  • Participate in GC procurement and oversee on-site progress.
  • Ensure quality, safety, and schedule adherence.
  • Review and approve pay applications and expenditures.
  • Track monthly cost updates from the GC.
  • Coordinate tenant improvements and closeout documentation.

Post-construction

  • Contract closeout and turnover to operations.
  • Commission building and equipment.
  • Train management and building maintenance personnel.
