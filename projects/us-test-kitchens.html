USA — Test Kitchens

$32M program delivering FM, UL, and FDA qualified test kitchens for McDonald’s and Wendy’s across multiple US locations.

Project Overview

CSC served as Owner’s Representative for a twelve site test kitchen program for McCain Foods and Conagra Foods in support of McDonald’s and Wendy’s menu R&D. We organized design approvals, packaged bids for qualified contractors, and provided oversight for safety, schedule, cost forecasting, and construction through equipment commissioning and turnover for intended use. Our team coordinated preconstruction and closeout with local municipalities across all locations. The work required careful management of fire code and FDA compliance with documented sign-offs at each phase.

Program Value and Scale

  • Value: Approx. $32M aggregate across 12 facilities.
  • Types: Commercial test kitchens with custom lineups and utilities.
  • Phasing: Staggered delivery with coordinated permitting and inspections.

Key Deliverables

  • FM, UL, and FDA qualified installations with documented approvals.
  • Equipment commissioning, QA/QC, and turnover packages.
  • Bid and vendor coordination for national brands and local jurisdictions.

Execution

  • Owner’s Representative and Design-Build oversight
  • Permitting, inspections, and multi-jurisdiction coordination
  • Schedule, quality, safety, and cost control
