Waco, Texas — Aluminum Can Production Facility

$61M Design/Build aluminum can production and distribution facility.

Image: Google Earth view of the facility.

Project Overview

Design/Build program for a new aluminum can production plant with integrated distribution. CSC managed the design and engineering, day-to-day construction activities, scheduling, safety planning and procedures, and the bidding and buyout of the project. Throughout delivery, CSC coordinated with the Waco City building and zoning departments, organizing inspections and quality control to ensure a compliant, on-schedule turnover.
