St. Louis, Missouri — Clean Room & Office Buildout

$3.4M computer/tech clean room (~2,500 SF) with nine additional floors of buildout.

Project Overview

CSC oversaw design and contractor prequalification for this $3.4M clean room and office program at 800 Market Street, St. Louis. The work included a ~2,500 SF custom clean room with specialized fire protection, mechanical, and electrical systems, plus nine floors of tenant buildout. CSC developed safety policies and procedures while ensuring the remaining 22 floors stayed operational. The project achieved a Recordable Incident Rate and Lost Workday Case Incident Rate of 0.00, with 24-hour work coordinated and delivered under budget and on schedule.
