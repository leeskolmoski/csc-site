San Bernardino, California — Wells Fargo Development

A coordinated multi-scope program: campus support for a high-security office, two parking structures, a ground-up Panera Bread, a retail shell for future tenants, a clean-room tenant improvement inside the campus, and additional neighborhood retail.

Project Overview

This corner of East Brier Drive wasn’t a single project—it was a little district that came together in phases. We supported Wells Fargo’s high-security office and delivered the two garages that serve it. Around the campus, our team built a full ground-up Panera Bread, delivered a retail shell so future tenants could move fast, and completed a clean-room TI inside the Wells Fargo space. We ran owner’s rep/design-build oversight, kept permitting and inspections on schedule, and made sure security and systems vendors worked cleanly with the builders at every handoff.

What we delivered

  • Two multi-level parking structures (photos below).
  • Panera Bread: full ground-up delivery.
  • Strip-mall retail: shell for future tenant improvements.

  • Miscellaneous retail (Wingstop, Subway, and others).
