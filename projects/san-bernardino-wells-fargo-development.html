San Bernardino, California — Wells Fargo Development

Multi-scope program: high-security office support, dual parking structures, ground-up Panera Bread, multi-tenant retail shell, clean-room tenant improvement, and additional retail units.

Project Overview

CSC provided comprehensive Owner’s Representative and design-build oversight for the San Bernardino Wells Fargo Development. The program encompassed delivery of a high-security office building, two cast-in-place parking structures, a ground-up Panera Bread, a multi-tenant retail shell, an $8.2M clean-room tenant improvement, and miscellaneous retail units. Responsibilities included municipal permitting, contractor coordination, security and building-systems integration, phased turnovers, and quality, cost, and schedule control across all scopes.

Key Deliverables

  • High-security office campus support
  • Two multi-level parking structures
  • Panera Bread: ground-up development
  • Retail center shell for future tenant improvements
  • Clean-room tenant improvement within Wells Fargo campus
  • Additional retail including Wingstop, Subway, and others
