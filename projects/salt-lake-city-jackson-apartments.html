Salt Lake City, Utah · Jackson Apartments

$34,000,000 · 70-unit residential renovation with seismic and structural upgrades.

Project Overview

Renovation of the historic Jackson Apartments, originally built in 1920. CSC acted as general contractor for seismic and structural upgrades, tying together two existing buildings into a modernized 70-unit residential facility. The project included intensive structural retrofitting and modernization while preserving historic character.
