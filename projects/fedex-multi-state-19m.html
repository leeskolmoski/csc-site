FedEx — Multi State Distribution Upgrades

$19M total program value

Overview

Program of site and building upgrades for multiple FedEx distribution facilities across several states. Work included dock expansions, yard reconfiguration, MEP and low voltage improvements, and interior refreshes completed while facilities remained operational. CSC provided owner’s representative and CM support, coordinating phased work to avoid impact to throughput and safety.
