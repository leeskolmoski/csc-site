Irapuato, Mexico · Distribution and Storage

$32,900,000, 274,000 ft² facility delivered as a turnkey project from concept to completion.

Overview

CSC ran the developer prequalification process, including site evaluations and lease negotiations with nine developers, then oversaw design and onsite construction from concept to completion. Specifications for ConAgra Foods were developed, and onsite safety policies and procedures were implemented.

Scope

  • Ambient and cold storage distribution
  • Turnkey developer selection and contracts
  • Design management and construction oversight

Delivery

  • Concept through commissioning
  • Owner’s Representative and Construction Management support
  • Safety program design and implementation
