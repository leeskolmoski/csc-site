St. Louis, Missouri — Test Kitchens and Clean Rooms

$8.6M combined program at 800 Market Street. $3.4M computer/tech clean rooms with 9 floors of buildout, plus a $5.2M office and culinary test kitchens with tasting lab.

Project Overview

CSC served as owner’s representative and CM support for dual scopes at 800 Market Street. Scope one was a computer and tech clean room of approximately 2,500 square feet with custom fire protection, mechanical, and electrical systems, delivered alongside nine floors of tenant buildout. Scope two was an office and culinary test kitchens program that included a tasting lab and flexible collaboration areas. CSC led design oversight and contractor prequalification with bidder evaluations, coordinated 24 hour work to keep the remaining 22 floors open, and developed the safety policies and procedures that supported both scopes. The program finished under budget and within schedule targets with a Recordable Incident Rate and Lost Workday Case Incident Rate of 0.00.
