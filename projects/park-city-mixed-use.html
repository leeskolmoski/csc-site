Park City, Utah · Mixed-Use Development

$44,000,000 design/build mixed-use project: 5-story commercial/residential with 3-story subgrade parking.

Project Overview

CSC served as General Contractor for this five-story mixed-use commercial and residential facility in Park City, Utah. The $44,000,000 design/build program included three levels of subgrade parking, extensive coordination with municipal authorities, and integration of tenant improvements. Work emphasized structural quality, schedule certainty, and safe delivery in a constrained mountain environment.
