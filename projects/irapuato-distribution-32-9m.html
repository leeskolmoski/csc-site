Irapuato, Mexico — Distribution & Storage

$32,900,000, 274,000 ft² facility delivered as a turnkey project from concept to completion.

Project Overview

CSC led a greenfield distribution warehouse in Irapuato with ambient and cold storage, cross-dock capability, and expanded truck yard improvements. We ran the developer prequalification process (site evaluations and lease negotiations with nine developers) and then oversaw design and onsite construction from concept to commissioning. Specifications for ConAgra Foods were developed and implemented, and onsite safety policies and procedures were designed and put into practice. CSC provided Owner’s Representative and Construction Management support across permitting, utilities, trade buyout, and phased startup, maintaining export compliance and safety standards throughout.

Program Value & Scale

  • Facility: 274,000 ft² distribution and storage.
  • Value: $32,900,000.
  • Site: Yard upgrades, paving, fencing, and lighting.

Key Deliverables

  • Ambient and cold storage zones with cross-dock layout
  • Refrigeration and Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing integration, dock equipment
  • Commissioning and operator training
  • ConAgra Foods specification development

Execution

  • Owner’s Rep and Construction Management support
  • Developer prequalification and contract strategy
  • Preconstruction planning, trade buyout, long-lead tracking
  • Phased turnover for early racking and stocking
  • Safety program design and implementation
