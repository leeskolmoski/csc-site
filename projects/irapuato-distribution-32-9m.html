Irapuato, Mexico · Distribution & Storage

$32,900,000, 274,000 ft² facility delivered as a turnkey project from concept to completion.

Project Overview

CSC led a greenfield distribution warehouse in Irapuato with ambient and cold storage, cross-dock capability, and expanded truck yard improvements. We ran the developer prequalification process (site evaluations and lease negotiations with nine developers) and then oversaw design and onsite construction from concept to commissioning. CSC developed construction specifications for ConAgra Foods and were implemented. CSC developed onsite safety policies and procedures. CSC provided Owner’s Representative and Construction Management (CM) support across permitting, utilities, trade buyout, and phased startup, maintaining export compliance and safety standards throughout.

Program Value & Scale

  • Facility: 274,000 ft² distribution and storage.
  • Value: $32,900,000.
  • Site: Yard upgrades, paving, fencing, and lighting.

Key Deliverables

  • Ambient and cold storage zones with cross-dock layout
  • Refrigeration and MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing) integration, dock equipment
  • Commissioning and operator training
  • ConAgra Foods specification development

Execution

  • Owner’s Representative and Construction Management (CM) support
  • Developer prequalification and contract strategy
  • Preconstruction planning, trade buyout, long-lead tracking
  • Phased turnover for early racking and stocking
  • Safety program design and implementation
