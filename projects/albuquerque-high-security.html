Albuquerque, New Mexico — High-Security Government Office

$34M Department of the Interior office with advanced security and controlled construction.

Project Overview

$34M high-security government office facility for the Department of the Interior in Albuquerque, New Mexico. CSC served as Owner’s Representative overseeing design, bid, and construction phases with strict security protocols. Scope included hardened envelope, secure MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing) systems, redundant power, and advanced surveillance integration.

Program Value & Scale

  • Facility: Department of the Interior secure office
  • Value: $34M
  • Duration: ~20 months

Key Deliverables

  • Hardened building envelope with blast-resistant glazing
  • Secure MEP and redundant systems
  • Advanced surveillance and access control
  • Commissioning with federal and state review

Execution

  • Owner’s Representative, Construction Management support
  • Strict procurement and contractor vetting
  • Phased security integration
  • Zero breaches during execution
