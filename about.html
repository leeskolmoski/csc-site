Construction Services Consulting, Inc. (CSC) represents owners on complex work. We coordinate design, contractors, and documentation so projects are delivered safely, on budget, and on schedule.

Who we are

CSC was founded by three partners with decades of construction and owner-side experience across industrial, food and beverage, cold storage, and commercial development. Our work is documentation-first and safety-forward, because many of our clients operate facilities that cannot shut down or accept rework.

We are a family-run, U.S.-based firm that can support domestic and international programs. Our role is to protect the owner’s interests from concept through turnover.

How we work

CSC fits in where the owner needs a single point of accountability. We help define the program, build the team, manage the design process, and keep contractors coordinated in the field.

  • Owner’s representation for multi-stakeholder programs
  • Preconstruction, budgeting, and constructability reviews
  • Schedule, cost, and quality tracking
  • Field photo and progress documentation
  • Closeout packages for future maintenance

The goal is simple. Deliver the project in a way that lets the owner operate the facility immediately.

Safety, documentation, and risk

Many of the programs CSC has supported required strict safety, incident reporting, and inspection documentation. Our process centers on building that documentation as the work is being done.
