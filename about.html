About CSC

More than 100 years of combined experience from three partners. We guide projects from concept to completion with precision, safety, and care.

Who we are

CSC was founded by experienced industry leaders who saw the need for an owner-focused partner in the construction process. Our role is to simplify complexity, advocate for the client, and ensure projects are delivered on time, on budget, and to specification.

We free up leadership to focus on operations and future sites, while CSC manages the details — concept to completion — with uncompromising attention to safety and quality.

Our approach

Precision

Every project is planned, coordinated, and tracked with rigorous detail. We hold contractors, vendors, and ourselves accountable to the schedule and the budget.

Safety

We build safety into every stage. From permitting through commissioning, CSC’s protocols ensure compliance and protect both people and operations.

Care

Relationships matter. CSC provides clear communication, responsive problem-solving, and a commitment to client priorities throughout the entire process.
