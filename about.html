Who We Are

With over sixty years of combined experience, we help corporations and developers in the U.S. and abroad turn complex plans into high-performing, easy-to-maintain facilities.

Keys to a Successful Project

Every project begins with clarity. We work with you to define scope, schedule, quality standards, and budget from the start. By doing the heavy lifting in pre-construction—reviewing drawings, refining specifications, and building on paper first—we minimize surprises and reduce costly change orders. The result is a project that stays on budget and on track.

Our Project Management Philosophy

Our approach is disciplined but practical. We use complete, accurate contracts to keep procurement smooth. We plan 60–90 days ahead, hold teams accountable, and monitor quality, cost, and schedule at every step so you get exactly what you pay for.

What We Prioritize

We believe in efficient, phased delivery that is easy to maintain over the life of your asset. Local requirements are understood and met, and the final result is an organized, coordinated facility that holds value for decades.

How CSC Helps

  • Site selection & due diligence: Plan reviews for cost, function, quality, and maintenance.
  • Municipal coordination: Navigate permitting, zoning, and utility constraints.
  • Streamlined construction: Continuous improvement and reliable planning data to stay on schedule.
  • Leadership focus: We free your team to concentrate on operations and growth while we manage the build.

Team Advantages

Our team brings international experience across development, design, and construction. We are fluent in diverse municipal requirements and have strong networks of quality contractors both in the U.S. and abroad. This allows us to build with confidence and deliver results that work anywhere.
