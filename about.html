Who We Are

CSC is a construction management company run by two partners with more than six decades of experience. We partner with corporations and developers to deliver efficient, safe, high quality projects in local and international markets.

Keys to every successful project

Four pillars

Scope, schedule, quality, budget.

80/20 rule

Do the work on paper first. Changes during construction cost at least 20 percent more.

Plan ahead

Work 60 to 90 days ahead with clear accountability and reporting.

Management approach: complete and accurate contracts, full drawings and specs to reduce change orders, plan ahead, hold teams accountable, and monitor quality, cost, and schedule.

Assumed priorities

  • Efficient, streamlined delivery.
  • Accurate representation of client products and services.
  • Compliance with all local requirements.
  • Facilities that are easy to maintain and cost effective to operate.
  • Assets that hold long term value.

CSC team advantages

  • Internationally experienced experts across development, design, and construction.
