Who We Are

Two partners with more than six decades of experience. We partner with corporations and developers in the U.S. and internationally to turn complex plans into successful facilities.

Keys to every successful project

  • Define Scope, Schedule, Quality, Budget up front.
  • Do the heavy lifting in pre-construction to control cost and change orders.
  • Design to budget and build on paper first to reduce changes.

Project management philosophy

  • Effective procurement with complete, accurate contracts.
  • Ensure drawings and specifications are complete to minimize changes.
  • Plan 60 to 90 days ahead, then follow the plan and hold teams accountable.
  • Monitor quality, cost, and schedule so clients get what they pay for.

Assumed priorities

  • Efficient, phased delivery with an organized, coordinated process.
  • Local requirements understood and met, facilities easy to maintain.
  • Completed assets that hold value over time.

How CSC helps

  • Site selection and due diligence, with plan reviews for cost, function, quality, and maintenance.
  • Municipal coordination and advantageous contracting.
  • Streamlined construction with continuous improvement and reliable planning data.
  • Free leadership to focus on operations and future sites.

Team advantages

  • International experience across development, design, and construction.
  • Fluent with diverse municipal requirements and market conditions.
  • Strong networks of quality contractors in the U.S. and abroad.
