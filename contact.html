Start a conversation

To help us respond quickly and accurately, share your goals, target schedule, budget range, scope highlights, location, and any known constraints (utilities, permitting, site access, supply chain, or operational windows).

What to include

  • Project type and objectives
  • Location and desired start or finish dates
  • Budget range and funding approach
  • Key stakeholders and decision timeline
  • Any known constraints or risks

What to expect

We will review your information, identify the fastest compliant path, and outline next steps. That could be due diligence, design coordination, procurement planning, or permitting strategy.

Next steps

After our initial call, CSC can prepare a tailored scope and engagement plan. We can act as your Owner’s Representative, provide Construction Management (CM) support, or build a hybrid model for your program.
